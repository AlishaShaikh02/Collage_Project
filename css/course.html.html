World class library

The central Library named as "Shrimant Shivajiraje Naik Nimbalkar Library" of Mudhoji College, Phaltan was established in the year 1957, since then library has made consistent progress in terms of collection of books, rare reading material, periodicals, e-resources and services. The college has separate library building with an extension building. The library has stack section, manuscript section, librarian's office and books circulation section. The library is also connected with the internet facility. The library provides facility to outdoor readers.

Testy and Healthy Food

College Canteen is located on the beautiful site of the campus. It has fresh surrounding with plenty of pollution free air and plenty of availability of Sun light. Canteen is divided into three different sections and each one is reserved for the Staff, gent students and lady students. Canteen authority serves good quality and fresh food items to the consumers. There is the separate Canteen Committee to have a timely watch on preparation and quality of the products.

About Us

Mudhoji College Phaltan is 65 years old co-educational grant-in-aid college started in the year 1957.The institution caters to the need around 4000 agrarian
based rural students especially girls, who constitute 64 % of the total strength. The college offers a range of six programs at UG level which includes B.Sc.
(in chemistry, physics, zoology, botany, statistics, electronics and maths subject), B.A. (in Marathi, Hindi, English, History, Politics, Geography, Economics, philosophy subject),
B.Com., B.C.S., B.C.A., and B.B.A. and three PG program M.A.,in Marathi, Hindi and English M.Sc. in Chemistry and M.Com. And M.A., M.Com, M.Sc. Distance mode of Shivaji University and YCMOU.
